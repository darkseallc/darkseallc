About Dark Sea LLC

Professional e-commerce solutions based in the heart of Arizona

Dark Sea LLC is a Tucson, Arizona-based e-commerce company specializing in professional online sales solutions. Under the leadership of owner Omer Faruk Kizildeniz, we provide expert services across leading e-commerce platforms including Amazon and Etsy.

Our Mission: To empower businesses with innovative strategies and expert guidance, helping them navigate the complex waters of digital commerce and achieve sustainable growth in the online marketplace.

From the vibrant city of Tucson, we extend our reach globally, serving as a trusted partner in the ever-evolving world of e-commerce. Our commitment to quality products and exceptional customer service sets us apart in the industry.
